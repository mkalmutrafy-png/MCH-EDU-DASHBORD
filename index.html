Program Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a leading SCFHS-accredited training center producing competent, safe, and innovative obstetricians and gynecologists for the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Mission

To graduate competent, safe, skilled, and knowledgeable specialists capable of functioning independently in obstetrics and gynecology, demonstrating clinical excellence, professionalism, patient safety commitment, and lifelong learning.
